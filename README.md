# rtame 🪔

*Cosmic-order-truth-righteousness as a state a life is aligned to — and received as a gift, not built as a virtue: the Vedic register of the one order that holds.*

rtame is two true roots welded into one word: the Vedic Sanskrit **ṛta** (ऋत — one of the oldest Indo-European religious concepts, the cosmic-order-truth-righteousness the gods Mitra and Varuṇa uphold against *anṛta*, anti-truth and disorder), and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So rtame is ṛta-alignment carried as an ordinance — not the cosmic fact alone (the sun rising, the seasons turning), but the human's reception of the order those facts manifest. It reaches back past *dharma* to the older Vedic stratum on purpose, to honor the layer where the recognition first stood.

rtame names a **grade** of rightness, not a single verdict: the kind where truth and justice and righteousness and the world's plain regularity are one given fabric a life can step into. Its light is the floor under conscience and cosmos at once, and its immunity to pride — the order is *received*, so alignment can never curdle into self-congratulation. Its shadow is that gift turning to possession: *I am aligned to the order* sliding into *I am the order*, the tyrant who calls his own will the law of the world. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.20/10** (Specialized).
